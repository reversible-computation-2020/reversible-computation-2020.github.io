Registration

Registration

Registration can be completed via the EPFL Pay Online system by clicking here.

Notice that the registration system also offers the possibility to register for the International Workshop on Logic & Synthesis, which is co-located with RC 2019.

Registration Fee

This package will include a copy of the proceedings, all lunches and coffee breaks throughout the conference, the social dinner, and the delegate registration fee.

  • Early Registration (until May 30): 280 CHF
  • Late Registration (from May 31): 330 CHF

Extra Dinner Ticket

An additional dinner ticket can be obtained for 110 CHF CHF through the registration system.

Social Hike

We are organizing a light social hike on Sunday, June 23, 2019, the day before the conference through the Lavaux vinyards. It is a common social event together with the participants from the IWLS workshop. The hike is unofficially organized and possible public transportation costs are not covered by the official registration fee. Nevertheless, please indicate that you'd like to join the hike by crossing the item in the registration form.
