Location

Conference Venue

RC 2020 will be held in Oslo, Norway and hosted by the University of Oslo.

The conference will be held at the Institute for Informatics, University of Oslo, Norway. All talks will be held in the Smalltalk lecture hall (room 1416, floor 1 of Ole Johan Dahls Hus, right next to subway station Forskningsparken).

By air

The closest airport is Oslo Gardermoen (OSL) with many European and some intercontinental flights. Gardermoen is connected to Oslo by direct train connection (23 minutes).

Some low-cost airlines travel to Torp (TRP), which is connected to Oslo via train (approx. 90min - it is advisable to check train connections before booking very early or late flights).

By ship

There are ferry connections between Oslo and Denmark (Copenhagen, Frederikshavn) and Germany (Kiel).

Questions

If you have any question, please feel free to write to the local organizers through info@reversible-computation.org.
