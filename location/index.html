Location

RC 2019 will be held in Lausanne, Switzerland and hosted by the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). Lausanne is best accessible from Geneva Airport or by train.

Lausanne is notable for hosting the International Olympic Committee. It is situated on the shores of Lake Geneva (Lac Leman).

By air

The closest airport is Geneva Airport (GVA) with many European and some intercontinental flights. Lausanne can be reached with a direct train from Geneva Airport within one hour. Alternative airports are Zurich Airport (ZRH) and Basel Airport (BSL), which are connected to Lausanne via train (train ride is approximately 2.5 hours).

By train

Lausanne's central station (Lausanne Gare) has connections to Switzerland, France, and Italy. From Lausanne Gare take the metro M2 to Lausanne-Flon, and from Lausanne-Flon the metro M1 to EPFL.

By car

Lausanne is linked via the motorways A1 and A9 between Geneva and Bern.
